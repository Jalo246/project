Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send a document?
Navigate to the Send Document page from the sidebar and fill out the required fields. You can attach files up to 20MB in size and select multiple recipients from your contacts list.
How do I track my documents?
Click on Track Documents in the sidebar to view the status of your sent and received documents. You can filter by status, date, or recipient to quickly find the documents you're looking for.
Can I request electronic signatures?
Yes, when sending a document, check the "Request Signature" box and mark the areas where signatures are needed. Recipients will receive a notification and can sign electronically before sending the document back.
